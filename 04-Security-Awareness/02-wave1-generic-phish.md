**Phase:** 4 — Security Awareness & Phishing Program

**Date:** January 2026
**Template:** "Your package is waiting — confirm delivery" (FedEx-themed generic phish)

## Campaign Statistics

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Emails sent | 200 |

## Key Findings

1. **Baseline click rate: 27.3%** — slightly below industry average (30%) but higher than targeted 25%
2. **Finance clicked at 41.7%** — highest of all departments and most dangerous (wire transfer access)
3. **Only 6.1% reported** — significantly below target. Most users either clicked or ignored without reporting
4. **Executives performed well** — 25% click rate with strong 25% report rate (most security-aware group)
5. **Time to click averaged 4.2 hours** — indicates users opened during work hours and clicked without scrutiny

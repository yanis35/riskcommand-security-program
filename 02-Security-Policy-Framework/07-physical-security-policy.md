# Physical Security Policy

**Document ID:** POL-06
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-15
**Approved By:** Facilities
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

Protect NexPay's physical assets, personnel, and information from physical threats including unauthorized access, theft, tampering, and environmental hazards.

---

## 2. Scope

All NexPay offices, data centers, server rooms, and any location where NexPay equipment or data is stored.

---

## 3. Policy Requirements

### 3.1 Physical Access Control
- All facility entrances must be secured with electronic access control (badge or biometric).
- Visitors must sign in, present government ID, be escorted by an employee, and wear a visitor badge.
- Access badges must not be shared, loaned, or displayed publicly.
- Lost badges must be reported immediately and deactivated within 1 hour.
- Access rights must be revoked within 4 hours of employee termination.

### 3.2 Server Rooms & Network Closets
- Access to server rooms limited to authorized IT/Security personnel only.
- Multi-factor authentication required for server room access (badge + PIN or biometric).
- All server room access logged and reviewed monthly.
- Environmental controls: temperature monitoring, fire suppression, humidity control, UPS backup.

### 3.3 Workstation Security
- All workstations must lock automatically after 5 minutes of inactivity.
- Screens must be positioned to prevent shoulder surfing of sensitive information.
- Laptops must be physically secured with cable locks when in untrusted locations.
- Clean desk policy: No sensitive documents left on desks overnight. All documents locked in drawers.

### 3.4 Asset Disposal
- All equipment containing storage media must be securely wiped or physically destroyed before disposal.
- Certificate of destruction required for all decommissioned storage devices.
- Asset disposal logs must be maintained and reviewed quarterly.

### 3.5 Environmental & Safety
- Emergency exits must remain unobstructed and clearly marked.
- Fire extinguishers, first aid kits, and AEDs must be accessible and inspected annually.
- Evacuation drills conducted semi-annually.
